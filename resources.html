Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Mainland and Freezone? ▼
Mainland companies can trade anywhere in the UAE and internationally. Freezone companies are restricted to trading within the freezone or internationally, but offer 100% foreign ownership and tax benefits.
How long does it take to set up a company? ▼
Typically, a business license can be issued within 3-5 working days, depending on the activity and approvals required.
Can I get a residency visa with my company? ▼
Yes, structuring a company allows you to apply for investor and employee residency visas.
